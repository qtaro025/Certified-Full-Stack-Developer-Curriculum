Strawberry Souffle Pancakes

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making Strawberry Souffle Pancakes. This recipe is compliments of cookpad.com user Niki@NikiTh. The recipe is for 1 serving, so if more than one serving is desired, multiply the ingredients to accommodate the number of people.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 10 ml milk
  • 2 g vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 g sugar
  • 3 g milk powder
  • 15 g flour
  • 70 g egg white
  • 20 g sugar
  • Pinch cream of tartar
  • Whipped cream and strawberry powder

Instructions

  1. Slightly beat egg yolk, milk and 3 g of sugar. Add vegetable oil, vanilla. Beat well.
  2. Add sifted flour and milk powder. Stir gently. Set the batter aside.
  3. Prepare meringue by beating egg white until started foamy. Add in cream of tartar. Continue beating and add sugar in 3 portions until stiff peak is formed.
  4. Take some of meringue mix into egg yolk batter. Pour the mixture to meringue and fold gently until everything combine.
  5. Use ice cream scoop to shape the batter on low heated pan. Drop some water on the pan and cover with lid for 5 minutes. Flip the batter and cook for another 5 min. Serve warm with fresh strawberry and whipped cream
